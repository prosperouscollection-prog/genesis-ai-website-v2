Privacy

Privacy Policy

Genesis AI Systems takes privacy seriously. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. If you have questions, email us at info@genesisai.systems.

What We Collect

  • Name and phone number provided during a demo or onboarding call
  • Call recordings and transcripts generated by your AI receptionist
  • Basic contact information you provide when booking a call or purchasing a plan

How We Use It

  • To set up and operate your AI receptionist
  • To deliver call summaries after each call
  • To follow up with you about your account
  • We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use it for advertising.

Call Recordings and Transcripts

  • Recordings and transcripts are processed to generate your call summaries
  • They are stored only as long as needed to deliver your service
  • You can request deletion of your call data at any time by emailing info@genesisai.systems

Your Rights

  • You can request access to your data at any time
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time
  • You can opt out of communications by replying to any email or contacting us directly

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email info@genesisai.systems or write to us at: Genesis AI Systems, 28475 Greenfield Road, Suite 113 7709, Lathrup Village, MI 48076.
